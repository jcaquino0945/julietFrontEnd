Frequently Asked Questions

Just follow these simple steps in ordering your desired item.
  1. Go to our website.
  2. Choose a product you want to buy then click "Add to cart."
  3. Proceed to checkout. Select Cash on Delivery / Bank Deposit / GCash as Payment option.
  4. Enter your complete shipping details.
  5. That's it! we'll get notified and we'll process your order for shipment.

  • Once order has been checked out, cancellation is not possible on customer's end. You can send us a message on Facebook to cancel your order.
  • We can cancel your order as long as the item was not shipped and tracking number is not yet generated.

  • We accept Cash on Delivery and you can also pay through GCash.
  • You may also use Bank Deposit. Online transfers are also accepted.

  • Shipping will take 3-14 days depending on your location.
  • Please take note that our shipping and delivery service is handled by a third-party courier, whether it would be J&T Express, LBC, GoGo Express, Ninja Van, etc. We do not hold or control the time of the delivery once it's in transit status.
  • The couriers do not deliver during holidays.
There are instances that shipping times may take longer than what we stated because of these following reasons:
  • Bottlenecks in shipping.
  • Incomplete shipping address.
  • Area not covered via COD or area is considered as remote areas.
  • Unreachable mobile number.

  • You can send us a message on Facebook if you would like to change or update something in your shipping information.
  • Please take note that once the order has been processed or shipped, we can no longer change anything in the shipping information.

  • You can send a message on Facebook and Instagram @juliet.mnl.ph.
