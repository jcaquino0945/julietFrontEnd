Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Go to our website.
  2. Choose a product you want to buy then click Add to Cart.
  3. Proceed to checkout.
  4. Enter your personal information and complete shipping details.
  5. Select Cash on Delivery, Bank Deposit, or GCash as Payment option.
  6. Place your order. We'll get notified and we'll process your order for shipment.
  7. Wait 3-7 days before you receive your order.

Once order has been checked out, cancellation is not possible on customer’s end. You can send us a message on Facebook to cancel your order. We can cancel your order as long as the item was not shipped and tracking number is not yet generated.

We accept Cash on Delivery and you can also pay through GCash. You may also use Bank Deposit. Online transfers are accepted.

Shipping will take 3-14 days depending on your location. Please take note that our shipping and delivery service is handled by a third party courier, whether it would be J&T Express, LBC, GoGo Express, Ninja Van, etc. We do not hold or control the time of the delivery once it’s in transit status. The couriers do not deliver during Holidays.

There are instances that shipping time may take longer than what we stated because of the following reasons:
  1. Bottlenecks in shipping.
  2. Incomplete shipping address.
  3. Area not covered via COD or area is considered as remote area.
  4. Unreachable mobile number.

You can send us a message on Facebook if you would like to change or update something in your shipping information. Please take note that once the order has been processed or shipped we can no longer change anything in the shipping information.

You can send a message on Facebook and Instagram @juliet.mnl.ph

Most of our products are made of stainless steel material. Material used is stated in every product’s description.

As with all jewelry that is not made of solid gold or silver, they will tarnish over time. They can last longer with proper care. Check out our “Jewelry Care” to know how.

Stainless steel jewelry are generally considered hypoallergenic. However, It is advisable to take them off before showering or sleeping.
